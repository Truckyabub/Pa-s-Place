# Figma Design System

## Product

Steady Voice AI Studio

## Brand Direction

- premium dark studio interface
- futuristic but readable
- DAW-inspired, not DAW-copied
- high contrast
- electric mint and amber accents
- legal-first and rights-aware presentation
