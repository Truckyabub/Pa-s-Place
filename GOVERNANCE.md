# Governance

## Purpose

Pa’s Place exists to honour George Alexander Carey, lovingly known as Pa George, through verified family stories, pool memories, sponsor recognition, and the Pa George 9-Ball Tournament legacy.

The project must not be redirected toward unrelated branding, outside campaigns, or content that does not belong to the Carey family tribute.

## Launch Control

Family review is required before public launch. No public launch may happen until the approved family steward confirms the content.

Major approval is required for major changes once the approved family steward or steward group is named. No one should be able to quietly redirect the mission or take control without documented approval.

## Major Decisions

Major decisions include:

- Public launch.
- Event archive updates.
- Sponsor recognition updates.
- Photo or memory publication.
- Contact information changes.
- Use of names, logos, public claims, or archive dates.
- Ownership or admin transfer.
- Adding or removing sections from the tribute site.

## Approval Standard

Major changes should require family approval from the approved steward or steward group once that group is formally named.

## Decision Log

All major decisions should be logged with:

- Date.
- Decision.
- People approving.
- Reason.
- Any links or evidence.
- Follow-up action required.

## Contributor Boundary

Helping with editing, advising, designing, sponsoring, or sharing feedback does not grant ownership or authority over the project. Ownership, stewardship, public launch, and access decisions require documented approval.
