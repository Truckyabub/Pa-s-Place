      Hard rules and prompt validation

      If a prompt asks for copyrighted lyrics, real-artist imitation, or trademark misuse, the app warns instead of pretending it is safe.

      Safety rules

      • Use only owned, licensed, public-domain, or original material.
      • Do not imitate living artists or clone real voices without explicit consent.
      • Do not scrape platforms, bypass DRM, or automate release actions.
      • Store a rights record for every asset, dataset, prompt, and export.
