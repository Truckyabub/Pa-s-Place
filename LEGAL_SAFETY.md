# Legal Safety

Steady Voice AI Studio is intentionally built around rights-first guardrails.

## Hard rules

- Do not train on copyrighted music unless the user owns the rights, has written permission, has a valid license, or the material is public domain.
- Do not clone or imitate a real artist's voice without explicit written consent.
- Do not generate "in the style of" a living artist.
- Do not reproduce copyrighted lyrics, melodies, recordings, samples, album art, logos, or trademarks.
- Do not scrape or automate platforms such as Suno, Udio, Spotify, YouTube, or Apple Music.
- Do not bypass paywalls, DRM, rate limits, or terms of service.

## Required warnings

The app should warn when a prompt appears to request:

- copyrighted lyrics
- real-artist imitation
- unauthorized voice cloning
- trademark misuse

## Commercial readiness

Before any release export, confirm:

- lyric ownership or license
- voice consent or ownership
- sample provenance
- dataset provenance
- artwork rights
- metadata accuracy
- distribution status

## Design intent

The app uses mock generation by default so the user can build workflows without claiming access to unauthorized model behavior.
