# Event Transparency

## Current Status

Pa’s Place does not process payments or monetary transactions through the website.

## Required Verification

Before any public event support details are published, verify:

- Event date.
- Event location.
- Sponsor names.
- Auction items, if any.
- Any approved charitable note.
- Any public statement about where support is going.

## Public Claims

No tax receipt or registered charity claim should be made unless legally confirmed.

No public fundraising claim should be made until the family has verified the wording and details.

## Tracking

Public updates should be honest, documented, plain, and easy for families to understand.

Suggested tracking fields after launch:

- Date.
- Source.
- Event name.
- Sponsor or supporter.
- Approved support note.
- Verification status.

## Payment Destination Protection

If the family ever decides to add a separate payment flow in the future, any destination change should require multi-person approval and a written decision log entry.
