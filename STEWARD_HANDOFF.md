# Steward Handoff

## Purpose

If the approved family steward ever wants to hand the project to another approved steward, the project should allow a safe, respectful, documented handoff.

## Required Handoff Steps

1. Family approval.
2. Written confirmation from the incoming steward.
3. Review of the mission and public commitments.
4. Access review for website, records, and any contact or archive tools.
5. Export of available audit logs and decision logs.
6. Confirmation of the publication process and contact process.
7. Transfer of repository, domain, or admin access only after confirmation.
8. Recommendation for legal review before any formal ownership transfer, if needed.
9. Written final handoff note signed or otherwise confirmed by the approved parties.

## Protection Standard

The handoff should be peaceful, humble, transparent, and family-first. It should prevent confusion, accidental control changes, and quiet redirection of the mission.
