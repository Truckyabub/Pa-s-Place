# Launch Checklist

Before public launch, confirm each of the following:

1. Confirm family approval.
2. Confirm life dates, story text, and archive wording.
3. Confirm photo permissions.
4. Confirm sponsor names and logo use.
5. Confirm tournament date, time, and location.
6. Confirm contact information.
7. Test mobile layout.
8. Test GitHub Pages publishing.

## Notes

- Keep the site static until all approvals are complete.
- Do not add private family data unless the privacy and safety implications are reviewed first.
- Keep any "Coming Soon" sections clearly labeled until the information is ready.
